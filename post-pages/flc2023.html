Being a student speaker at the 2023 FOBISIA Annual Leadership Conference
I had the privilege of being invited to the 2023 FOBISIA Annual Leadership Conference held in Kuala Lumpur to speak alongside five other students from international schools across KL. We shared our experiences as students with Head Teachers from British international schools across Asia. This opportunity to contribute to the dialogue on how schools can embrace change, collaboration and inclusivity was both an honour and responsibility.
As a student who is fortunate enough to be able to attend an international school, I have had the privilege of being able to learn in a very culturally diverse environment. I believe this has certainly shaped the way I view the world around me and the opportunities that I was exposed to growing up. For that, I am incredibly thankful.
