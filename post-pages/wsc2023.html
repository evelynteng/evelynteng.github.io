World Scholar's Cup Regional KL Round 2023
This was my second year as a WSC scholar, so I was a lot more excited than I was nervous. This was my first year as a Senior scholar which meant going up against people in Years 12 and 13, which was intimidating at first, but everyone was so friendly and passionate.
My team and I ended up with First Place and lots of trophies + medals. As always, I'm very grateful to have had this opportunity to compete and meet so many passionate and curious scholars!
