The Global BizTech Challenge 2023
A competition made by students, for students. Here's a bit about what this business and technology competition was about!
Project Details / Background
The Global BizTech Challenge 2023 was a student-led competition that involved high school students around the world pitching their business ideas based on the newest technologies. Participants from 27+ different countries attended webinars led by experts and industry professionals to gain insights on how to start a business and what makes an appealing business idea to investors. The participants' submissions and ideas were then judged by a panel of judges who have experience in creating or investing in technologies, and were provided with personal feedback so that this competition could act as the first step in making their business ideas a reality.
